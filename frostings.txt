Cream Cheese:Smooth, tangy, and sweet. A sumptuous cream cheese frosting flavored with vanilla bean or orange zest.
American Buttercream:Creamy and decadent, a classic buttercream frosting.
Italian Meringue Buttercream:Silky, buttery, melt-in-your-mouth. Not quite as sweet or heavy as a traditional American Buttercream.
White Mountain:A delicate, light-as-air frosting named for the mountain-like peaks it forms when beaten. Reminiscent of marshmallow cream.
Stabilized Whipped Cream:light and fluffy confection made with local, organic cream.
